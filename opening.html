KITS Postdoc Fellows

There are currently openings for KITS Postdoctoral Fellow positions. Welcome to check more information or apply for it via AJO if interested. Deadline for 2021 application: Jan. 31st, 2021.

The postdoc position is typically signed up for 2 years, with the possibility of one year extensions upon mutual agreements. Fellows would have full freedom on choosing topics of research and collaborations among all the KITS faculty members. You are also welcome to send inquiries to me (phys.huang.biao@gmail.com) or other faculty members.
